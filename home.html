About me

Join me on a heartfelt art journey through YouTube, where I revisit my childhood passion for drawing and painting. As a little girl, I immersed myself in creative pursuits, and art has always held a special place in my heart. Inspired by Pablo Picasso's words, "Every child is an artist; the problem is staying an artist when you grow up."

I embarked on a YouTube adventure in the wet winter of New Zealand. I can't wait to share my artistic explorations with you and discover the joy of creating together.

Easy 15-Minute Watercolor Coneflower

Loosen Up & Paint with Me

Too busy to make art lately?
This is your gentle nudge to press pause and pick up your brush.
In this calming 15-minute session, we’ll paint loose watercolor coneflowers—those cheerful blooms with bold centers and swooping petals
and finish them with relaxed ink lines and playful pen patterns.
Perfect for beginners, this tutorial is all about letting go,
trusting your brush, and simply enjoying the process.

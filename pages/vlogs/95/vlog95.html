15-Minute Watercolor Flowers

Creative Break for Busy People.

Have you ever had one of those days where everything feels rushed… and you realize you haven’t done a single thing just for yourself?
That was me this morning. My to-do list was long, my mind was buzzing, and I felt that quiet ache—the one that says, “I need a pause.”
So, I reached for my sketchbook, a few favorite colors, and told myself: Just 15 minutes.
Not for productivity. Not for perfection. Just to feel the brush in my hand and watch color unfold.
And you know what? It worked.
